Born to Create

I grew up in a small Egyptian city called El Mahalla El Kubra, where art was more than a hobby—it was our family's language.

My father was a fashion designer, my mother a businesswoman, and together they taught me two worlds that seemed far apart: creativity and commerce.

As a kid, I spent hours sketching, painting, and joining art competitions… but I also loved numbers.

Maybe that's why, deep down, I was never just an artist — I was a builder.

The Hustler Kid

Like every curious kid, I wanted to create something of my own.

I sold hand-drawn school timetables to classmates.
I raised pets hoping to trade them.
I fixed computers around my neighborhood.

Most of these experiments failed…
but they taught me something priceless —

I was never afraid to start again.

The Struggle Years

When I moved to Alexandria for university, I faced one of the hardest chapters of my life.

No home. No money. Twice robbed.

I thought I came here to be the Picasso of college, but instead, I found humility — surrounded by students far more talented than me.

I learned that talent alone means nothing.

Growth happens when you're surrounded by people who challenge you to become better.

The Power of Community

Living in the university dorm changed everything.

We shared art, ideas, and ambitions. Day by day, our skills grew together — and that's when I realized something powerful:

"Being around the right people can transform your entire path."

That idea became the seed of a dream: to build a real place where people could grow together — not just learn.

I called it "Shahin School."
I wrote the name on my wall, and promised myself one day it'll exist.

5000 Pounds and a Dream

After graduation, I worked in a game design company with one of the smartest entrepreneurs I've ever met.

He introduced me to the world of business — and I fell in love.

I saved 5,000 EGP — all I had — and rented a tiny place that became the first version of Shahin Academy.

That was the real beginning.

Months later, I won a national entrepreneurship competition, joined a program with the American University in Cairo, and for the first time…

I learned how to turn creativity into a system.

Building Four Worlds

Years passed, and my dream multiplied.

I built four ventures — Grand Shahin, Shahin Gallery, Shahin Academy, and Made in Shahin.

Each one a reflection of a different part of my journey — art, community, business, and education — all connected by one mission:

to help others start and grow what they believe in.

The 5000 Rule

No matter how much I grow, I never forget this number — 5,000.

Because that's where it all began.

I remind myself every day that if I lose everything, I'll only lose what I built after that number.

And what I gained — the courage, the experience, the faith — will always be worth far more.

That's what keeps me fearless.
That's what keeps me moving forward.

The Heart of Everything

But behind every venture, every late night, and every big decision, there's the most important part of my story — my family.

Tahany Mahmoud, my wife, partner, and the creative force behind our shared dreams. She's an architect and fashion designer who doesn't just support my vision — she shapes it. Together, we've built not just businesses, but a life that balances ambition with love.

Dawood, our 8-year-old mathematician and future engineer, who already sees the world in patterns and possibilities. He's winning robotics competitions, mastering tennis, and coding his way into tomorrow. Watching him solve problems reminds me daily that curiosity is the foundation of everything great.

Dalida, our 5-year-old gymnast and silver medalist, who shows me that courage isn't about the size of the stage — it's about the size of your heart. Her determination in gymnastics, earning a silver medal in Egypt's National Cup, teaches me that excellence starts early when passion meets practice.

They are my why.

Every business decision I make, every risk I take, every dream I chase — it all comes back to creating a world where they can thrive. A healthy, positive home isn't just the foundation of a happy family — it's the energy source for everything else.

Because when your personal life is strong, your professional life becomes unstoppable.

Business With Purpose

"Business With Shahin" isn't just a brand — it's a movement built on experience, failure, art, and resilience.

I started alone.
Now, I teach others to do it smarter.

"Because success isn't about money.
It's about learning, giving, and building something that lasts."
